Shield Damage Tolerance
(Float) Number of weak hits needed to shatter the shield (Setting to 0 causes it to shatter as soon as Sandbag hits it)

Default (Float): 6 (0x40C00000)
	/EndDescription
0

Shield Damage Tolerance Multiplier
(Float) Multiplier for shield damage tolerance. Number of weak hits needed to shatter the shield is calculated by Tolerance*Multiplier

Default (Float): 1 (0x3F800000)
	/EndDescription
